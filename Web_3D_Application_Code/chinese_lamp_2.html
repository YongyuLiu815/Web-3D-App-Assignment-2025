Model Introduction

The Chinese Vintage Palace Lantern embodies the essence of traditional craftsmanship and cultural heritage, featuring an elegant wooden frame with intricate carvings and delicate hand-painted patterns. Its hexagonal or octagonal structure, often adorned with auspicious motifs such as dragons, phoenixes, or floral designs, symbolizes prosperity and harmony. The lantern's translucent silk or rice paper panels emit a soft, warm glow, creating a serene and inviting ambiance. Perfect for classic interiors, tea houses, or cultural spaces, this exquisite lantern is not just a lighting fixture but a timeless expression of oriental artistry and refined elegance.
